# Mathetes — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026. Operated by Celestial Church of Christ Federal Students
Parish (CCCFSP), Federal University Oye-Ekiti (FUOYE), Nigeria ("we", "us")._

> **Note:** this is a thorough starting draft tailored to how Mathetes works.
> Have it reviewed by someone familiar with Nigeria's **NDPR** (and Google
> Play's requirements) before publishing. Host it at **`https://mathetes.live/privacy`**
> and use that URL in the Play Store listing.

## 1. Who this applies to
Mathetes is a private discipleship app for members of CCCFSP at FUOYE. You
create an account to use it. Access is restricted to approved members.

## 2. Information we collect
**You give us:**
- Account: name, email address, password (stored hashed by our auth provider).
- Profile: date of birth, gender, phone number, level/year, department, campus,
  and house fellowship. A profile photo only if you choose to upload one
  (photos are off by default; initials are shown otherwise).
- Content you create: chat messages, prayer requests, Ask-Pastor questions,
  notes, bookmarks, highlights, and reading-plan reflections.
- Circle teaching recordings, only when a Circle owner or admin visibly starts
  a recording during a live Circle meeting.
- Giving: if you give, the amount, fund, and transaction reference. **Card and
  bank details are entered on Paystack's secure page — we never see or store
  your card details.**

**Collected automatically:** basic app/usage and device information needed to
operate the service (e.g. notification token if you enable notifications). We do
not use third-party advertising or tracking.

## 3. Why we use it (and the legal basis)
- To run the app: deliver the daily Word, devotionals, Bible, reading plans,
  community chat, prayer wall, Ask Pastor, and giving.
- To route you to the right parish/campus/house and keep the community safe.
- For **pastoral care and oversight** (see §5).
- To process gifts you choose to make (via Paystack).
- To send you notifications you have enabled.

We rely on your consent (which you can withdraw), on the legitimate interests of
running a closed church community, and on what's necessary to provide a feature
you use (e.g. giving).

## 4. Circle meeting recordings
Circle meetings are not recorded automatically. When a Circle owner or admin
chooses to record a teaching, everyone in that meeting sees a clear recording
notice and Circle members receive a notification. The finished recording is
private to the Circle; it is stored in a private Cloudflare R2 bucket and played
through a short-lived access link. Leaving or being removed from a Circle removes
access to its recordings.

## 5. Pastoral oversight (important)
Mathetes is a discipleship tool with pastoral accountability built in:
- House leaders, disciplers, the pastor and administrators may see that
  conversations are happening and may oversee community activity within their
  care.
- **Private message content is not read routinely.** It is accessed only where a
  safety concern is raised or a message is reported.
- This is for spiritual care and safety, never surveillance or commercial use.
- Your reading-plan reflections are **private to you** unless you explicitly
  choose to share a reflection with your discipler.

## 6. What we share — and what we never do
- **We never sell your data** or share it for advertising.
- We share data only with the service providers that run the app (see §7), and
  within your parish as described above (e.g. your name/house in the member
  directory, your prayer requests with your house as you choose).
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect someone's safety.

## 7. Service providers
- **Supabase** — database, authentication, file storage, and APIs (data hosted
  on Supabase's infrastructure).
- **Resend** — sending account/confirmation emails.
- **Paystack** — processing gifts (only if you give). Subject to Paystack's own
  privacy terms.
- **Expo / app stores** — app delivery and (if enabled) push notifications.
- **LiveKit and Cloudflare R2** — private Circle call delivery and, when an
  authorised Circle admin starts recording, private teaching recording storage.

## 8. Storage and security
Data is stored with our providers and protected by row-level security so members
only access what they're permitted to. Access to private content is limited as
in §5. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect
your information.

## 9. Your choices and rights
- View and edit your profile in the app at any time.
- Photo upload is opt-in; you can remove your photo.
- Choose who can message you and manage privacy in Settings.
- Block, report, or mute from any chat.
- Request a copy or deletion of your data, or to close your account, by
  contacting a parish leader or us at the address below. Some records may be
  retained where required (e.g. giving records for accounting).
- At the end of an academic term, chat history is archived and then removed to
  keep the community fresh.

## 10. Children
Mathetes is intended for university students and parish members. It is not
directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information,
contact us and we will remove it.

## 11. Changes
We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will change. Continued use
after an update means you accept the revised policy.
